# Intent

## Core
This page is a digital front door that makes a funded quantum founder feel
they've found the private room where the quantum economy is actually being
built — and apply to enter it.

When it is just right, the reader feels quiet confidence — a private bank's
prospectus written by someone who speaks qubits. The physics is real, the
claims are receipts, nothing is shouting. A funded founder thinks: "finally,
a room built by someone who actually understands my world."

## Boundaries
- NOT a mass-market SaaS site. No growth-hack patterns: no popups, no
  countdown timers, no fake scarcity, no exclamation points, no emoji.
  Restraint is the brand.
- NOT a paywall funnel for cold traffic. Cold or unqualified visitors are
  never pushed to the $300 ask — they are routed to the free Signal tier.
  The application is for funded founders only.
- NOT a content site or web app. One landing page plus an application form.
  No blog, no member portal, no login, no dashboard — the product lives
  elsewhere; this page only converts.
- NOT hype or invented claims. No fabricated testimonials, member counts,
  or dollar values. Every number traces to the strategy document; honesty
  about what exists at launch is a feature.
